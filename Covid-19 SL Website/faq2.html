DO'S AND DONT'S DURING THE COVID-19 OUTBREAK

The coronavirus pandemic has taken everybody by alarm. Here is a list of things that you should not do to save the infection and panic about the disease from escalating further.

Learn More

WAYS TO PROTECT YOURSELF

Wear A Face Mask

Wear well-fitted face masks in public to reduce Covid-19 transmission and save lives, regardless of your vaccination status.

Learn More

Wash Your Hands

Wash your hands with soap and water to prevent the spread of germs during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Learn More

Avoid Close Contact

It is important to maintain a 6 foot distance in crowded places to slow the spread and transmission of COVID-19.

Learn More

MAIN SYMPTOMS?

COVID-19 affects everyone in different ways. Seek immediate medical attention if you have serious symptoms. Always call before visiting your doctor or health facility.

  • Fever
  • Tiredness
  • Dry Cough
  • Sore Throat
  • Aches and Pains
  • Shortness of Breath
